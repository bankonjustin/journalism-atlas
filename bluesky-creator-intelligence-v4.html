The Poly-Platform Layer

When Atlas maps a creator, we don't just record where they publish — we capture their full platform surface area. A newsletter writer who also runs a YouTube channel and a podcast isn't three separate creators. They're one editorial voice with three audience entry points. That's the intelligence that makes discovery, bundling, and recommendation actually work.

The Spidering Frontier

671 seeds · follows + followers + starter packs

The 671 creators on Bluesky aren't a list — they're a network. Each one follows and is followed by dozens of other journalists not yet in the Atlas corpus. Our spidering method uses the mapped corpus as seeds, then traverses their follows, followers, and curated starter packs to surface new creators who meet Atlas verification standards.

A single curated starter pack — Amy Westervelt's "Climate Journalists" list on Bluesky, 149 members — yielded more than a dozen net-new Atlas-eligible creators in one session. Every seed creator connects to more.

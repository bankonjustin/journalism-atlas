Section 7: Mission Statement

Independent journalism has a discovery problem.

Thousands of journalists have built sustainable, independent operations outside of legacy newsrooms. Newsletters, YouTube channels, podcasts, investigative websites — the ecosystem is vast and growing. But it's fragmented. Readers can't find it. Funders can't map it. The Atlas exists to fix that.

We verify every creator in the database, track them across platforms, and build tools that make the ecosystem legible — to everyone.

Verified
Every creator is editorially reviewed
We don't scrape. We evaluate. Every entry in the Atlas has been reviewed for editorial independence, journalistic quality, and platform presence.
Comprehensive
Newsletters, video, podcasts, social
The Atlas spans six platforms and tracks secondary presences. A newsletter writer who also has a YouTube channel appears in both contexts.
Growing
100+ new creators added per quarter
The ecosystem doesn't stand still. Neither does the Atlas. We add and verify new creators continuously, with a particular focus on underrepresented geographies and beats.

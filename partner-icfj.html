About ICFJ+

The International Center for Journalists (ICFJ+) is a global nonprofit that strengthens independent journalism by equipping journalists with the skills, tools, and networks they need to report freely and build sustainable careers. With a presence in more than 100 countries, ICFJ+ supports the journalists documenting the stories that matter most.

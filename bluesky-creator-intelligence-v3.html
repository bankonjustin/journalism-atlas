How We Spidered & What's Left

AT Protocol · 9 sessions · open flywheel

What worked

searchActors — no auth required. Single-concept queries work; multi-word specialty searches fail silently. Best: "investigative reporter," "independent journalist," "climate newsletter."
getFollows graph mining — seeded from Legum, Reed, Westervelt, Koebler, Kim Kelly, Perry, Rupar. Each yields 100 follows; filtered by bio + follower threshold.
getProfiles (batch) — 25 actors per call. Fast bulk follower count retrieval. Reliable.
getFollowers (S2) — Kim Kelly + Koebler follower lists. Surfaces community membership, not just outward network. Higher signal for beat communities.
Named-seed lookups (S2) — Direct handle resolution for beats where search returns empty. Found Soleil Ho, Patrick Wyman, Ed Yong this way.

What was blocked

searchPosts — 403 without auth. Key missing tool. Post-level search unlocks starter pack discovery, mentions, thread signal.
blueskystarterpack.com — 403. Directory blocked unauthenticated. Chrome confirms login-wall on post search.
